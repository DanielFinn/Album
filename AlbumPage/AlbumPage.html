Led Zepplin II

Led Zepplin

Release Date: October 22, 1969

Led Zepplin II was a commercial succes, and was the band's first Album to reach number one on the UK charts and the US. Since it's realease, various writers and music critics have citedLed Zepplin II as one of the most influential albums of all time.
